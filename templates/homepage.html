About Us

The Healthcare Management system is the inevitable part of the lifecycle of the modern medical institution. It automates numerous daily operations and enables smooth interactions of the users ie. both patients and doctors . Developing the Healthcare Management system software is a great opportunity to create the distinct, efficient and fast delivering healthcare model.

Patient

The patient module enables patients to search for their specialty and doctor or Health Check and book an appointment for a consultation or Health Check online. They can enter their complete details online and reduce the waiting time at the hospital.

Doctor

The doctor mdodule enables doctors to keep an eye on the number of appointments booked under them.
After physical chek-up / visit of a patient the doctor can even upload the required prescriptions , reports , additional details etc .
